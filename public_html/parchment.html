About Parchment Craft

Parchment or Pergamano came into my life because I wanted to make some nice invitations for our 25th wedding anniversary party.

I discovered it at a craft fair (hobby messe). I hadn't ever seen it before, and I liked what I saw; you get to buy all these cool tools!

Luckily, I was able to buy a starter's kit with instructions for a nice invitation, and after making 35 of them I got quite good at it. You need a steady hand, good eyesight and lots of light to do this work.

"Don't push too hard or you'll go right through!"

What I have made

Well, like I said, I made about 35 invitations, 74 place setting card, and have made several cards that I have given to people for birthdays and such. I hope to join a class some time and learn some new techniques.
