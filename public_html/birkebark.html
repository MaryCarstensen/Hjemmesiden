About the boxes

I saw an article in the local paper about an exhibit at the local library, and was inspiried to make my own. I was able to sign up for a workshop with the Husflid club in Randers.

This is also a handcraft from the past. Birch bark is some very interesting qualities. I believe that it is practically waterproof, and burns well too!
