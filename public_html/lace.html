About lacemaking

I discovered knipling when I came to Denmark the first time as an exchange student with AFS. I went to an evening class and learned the craft.

I would compare lacemaking to macrame; it is all about knots. There are two basic movements that your hands make with the bobbins (of thread). It is quite easy and logical. You just follow the pattern, and work where you can. I haven't made anything with really small thread.

"Hvor svært kan det være!"
      "Really, it's easy!"

What I have made

I have made several scarfs, some wall hangings, some small Christmas decorations, and some doilies. So far I have only made lace from patterns that other people have created. Someday I would like to make my own patterns.
